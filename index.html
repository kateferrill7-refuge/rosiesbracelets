From my hands to yours

How each bracelet gets made

1

I pick the beads

Everything starts at the bead shop or in my collection. I choose by color, feel, and how they play together — no formula.

2

String by hand

Each bead goes on by hand, one at a time. I lay out the pattern first, then work through it slowly so the spacing feels right.

3

Knot and finish

Double-knotted closures on elastic or clasp cord, depending on the design. I tug-test every single one before it ships.

4

Wrapped with care

Tissue paper, a little card, and if you asked — a handwritten note. Then off it goes.
